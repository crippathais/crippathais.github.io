Future & Past Meetings

The "Workshop of the of the Brazilian Initiative on Precision Medicine (BIPMed)" and the "Meeting of the Brazilian node of the Human Variome Project (HVP)" is where you can have an overview of the updates and perspectives of the Initiative. The event is designed for students, collaborators, researchers, and managers from public and private initiatives interested in the subject. It provides a discussion on data-sharing strategies, new technical developments, including security of data and infrastructure developed for integration with other national and international initiatives and other nationwide projects that could be integrated with BIPMed
